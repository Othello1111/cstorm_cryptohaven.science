linux-canadawest

linux-lisbon
linux-london
linux-paris
linux-rome

linux-switzerland

linux-useast

For Windows users, replace the "linux-" part of the hostname with "windows-"

linux-balancer.cstorm.pw (or .cryptostorm.net or .cryptostorm.org)
windows-balancer.cstorm.pw (or .cryptostorm.net or .cryptostorm.org)
